About Kafal Cooperative Society

Our journey, mission, and leadership

Our History

Founded in 1985, Kafal Cooperative Society has grown from a small community cooperative to one of the most trusted financial institutions in the region. With over three decades of service, we've built our reputation on integrity, innovation, and personalized service.

Today, we serve over 2 million customers through our network of 250+ branches and our award-winning digital banking platform.

Our Mission

At Kafal Cooperative Society, our mission is to empower individuals and businesses to achieve their financial goals through innovative cooperative banking solutions and exceptional service.

We believe in building lasting relationships with our customers by understanding their unique needs and providing tailored financial solutions that help them thrive.
